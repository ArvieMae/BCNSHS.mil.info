About BCNSHS

Bais City National Science High School (BCNSHS) is a public secondary school in Bais City, Negros Oriental. The school focuses on giving quality education especially in science, math, and research. It aims to guide students to discover their talents, improve their skills, and learn good values that they can use in life. BCNSHS is also a place where students are encouraged to join academic contests, leadership activities, and school events. Because of this, the school has produced many students who do well not only in their studies but also in serving others. As a student of BCNSHS, I can say that it is a school that really helps us grow and prepares us for the challenges of the future.
